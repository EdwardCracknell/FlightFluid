Built for Flyers and Coaches

🎯

Session Planner & Skill Tree

Create goal‑based plans with levelled progressions (belly → back → sit → head‑down). Auto‑split time into focused sets with rest and notes.

📹

Capture & Annotate

Record slow‑motion video, draw lines, and drop timestamps to mark entries, fall‑rate changes, docks, and exits.

🧠

AI Debrief

Computer‑vision highlights posture symmetry, centerline drift, and transition smoothness. Get a prioritized, actionable briefing for the next session.

📈

Metrics that Matter

Track speed bands, hold time on target, stability score, and transition efficiency with trendlines across flights.

🗓️

Booking & Logbook

Book coaches, manage blocks, and auto‑log minutes, drills, notes, and media in a searchable logbook.

🌐

Coach Marketplace

Find rated coaches by discipline and tunnel. Share plans and debriefs with students in one tap.

Last updated: Aug 2025

FlightFluid processes your account details and training data to provide coaching features. Videos are stored locally unless you enable cloud sync. Text prompts for AI debriefs may be sent to trusted providers; videos are not used to train models.

Data We Collect
