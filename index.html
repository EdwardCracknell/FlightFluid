Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create a FluidFlight account, we collect your email address, username, profile information, flying experience level, and training goals.

Video Content: We process videos you upload of your wind tunnel sessions for coaching analysis and feedback purposes.

Progress Data: We track your skill progression, completed tutorials, coaching feedback received, and achievement milestones.

2. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contract Performance: Providing coaching services, video analysis, and educational content
  • Legitimate Interest: App improvement, safety protocols, and community features
  • Consent: Marketing communications and optional features (where required by law)
  • Legal Obligation: Compliance with safety regulations and legal requests

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Coaching Services: Analyze your flying videos to provide personalized feedback and progression plans
  • Educational Content: Recommend appropriate tutorials and training materials based on your skill level
  • Progress Tracking: Monitor your skill development and provide achievement recognition
  • Community Features: Enable connections with other flyers and coaches
  • Safety & Support: Ensure safe flying practices and provide customer assistance
  • Service Improvement: Enhance our coaching methodologies and content quality

4. Video Processing & Storage

Coaching Analysis: Videos are reviewed by certified coaches who provide detailed feedback and annotations.

Storage Duration: Videos are retained for 90 days after analysis to allow for progress comparison and follow-up questions.

AI Assistance: We may use AI tools to assist coaches in identifying key technique points, but all feedback is reviewed and personalized by human experts.

Content Ownership: You retain full ownership of your flying videos. We only use them for providing coaching services.

Privacy Controls: You can delete your videos at any time, and choose whether to share them with the community.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information. Limited sharing occurs only in these circumstances:

  • Certified Coaches: Your videos and profile information with assigned coaches for analysis
  • Cloud Storage: AWS for secure video storage and streaming
  • Payment Processing: App Store, Google Play Store, and Stripe for subscriptions
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage data for app improvement (opt-out available)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by valid legal process or safety concerns

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data and coaching history
  • Correction: Update or correct your profile and skill information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Export your progress data and coaching feedback
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your information
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt-out of optional features and communications

Exercise Your Rights: Contact us at privacy@fluidflight.app or use in-app privacy controls.

7. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for video uploads and personal data
  • Access Controls: Strict coach verification and limited data access
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and penetration testing
  • Incident Response: 24/7 monitoring and breach notification procedures

8. Data Retention

Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion

Videos: Kept for 90 days after coaching analysis unless you delete them sooner

Progress Records: Maintained for the duration of your account to track improvement

Coaching Feedback: Stored permanently with your account for reference

9. Children's Privacy

FluidFlight requires users to be at least 13 years old. Users under 18 must have parental consent. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers
  • Processing only in countries with adequate data protection laws
  • Encryption and security measures regardless of location

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. We will notify you of significant changes via app notification or email.

12. Contact Us

For privacy questions or concerns:

Email: privacy@fluidflight.app
Support: support@fluidflight.app
